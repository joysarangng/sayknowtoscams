Anti-scam safeguards in Singapore are offered by different entities — from telcos, to banks, etc.

Secure your funds from scams

How you're protected today

✓

Removal of clickable links from SMSes

Banks will not send you clickable links via SMSes

✓

Emergency self-service "kill switch"

Activate the kill switch to suspend your bank account quickly, if you think your account has been compromised

✓

Restricted user access if dangerous apps are detected

If access to your bank account is restricted, check if you have installed any potentially dangerous apps (e.g. from unofficial sources)

✓

Default online transaction limit of $5,000 or less

Avoid increasing your transaction limit unnecessary, to limit losses if your account has been compromised

Opt in for greater security

☐

Activate "money lock" feature to secure funds

Consumers can now set aside a certain amount of funds to be protected from all digital transfers.

Activate for FREE now:

  • For DBS/POSB customers: Activate on digibank online or digiVault

  • For OCBC customers: Activate on your OCBC Digital app or learn more here

  • For UOB customers: Activate on your UOB TMRW app or learn more here
